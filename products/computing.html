Computing Products

Servers

File storage and sharing, email, web hosting, database management, and application hosting that are optimized for high performance, reliability, and availability, and are designed to handle large amounts of traffic and data.

Storage

Hard Disk Drives (HDDs), Solid-State Drives (SSDs), or Network-Attached Storage (NAS) devices.

SAN Switch

Provide high-speed connectivity between servers and storage devices, allowing multiple servers to access the same storage resources simultaneously.

Server Racks

Specialized enclosures designed to hold and organize servers, network devices, and other equipment in data centers, server rooms, or other IT environments.

NAS

Network-Attached Storage. Convenient and cost-effective way to provide centralized storage and file sharing services on a network.

DAS

Direct-Attached Storage. Simple and cost-effective way to add storage capacity to a single computer or server.

Workstation

Specialized computer designed for demanding applications that require a lot of processing power, memory, and storage.

PC Desktop

Offers a wide range of capabilities and customization options. Ideal for users needing power and reliability for gaming, editing, or intensive applications without requiring portability.

Laptop

Portable computers for users who prefer the convenience and portability of a built-in display, keyboard, and trackpad.

All-in-One

A combination of style, convenience, and functionality. Perfect for users who want a sleek desktop with easy setup and a minimalist workspace.

Convertibles

Also known as "2-in-1" or "hybrid" laptops. Designed with a rotating or detachable screen to switch between laptop and tablet modes, offering flexibility and versatility.

Convertibles

Commonly referred to as a "2-in-1" or "hybrid" laptop. Convertibles typically have a hinge mechanism that allows the screen to be rotated 360 degrees or detached from the keyboard base to function as a standalone tablet. This design allows users to switch between a laptop and a tablet mode based on their needs, providing greater flexibility and versatility compared to traditional laptops or tablets.

Tablets

Designed to be portable, convenient, lightweight and slim. A tablet is a wireless, portable personal computer with a touchscreen interface.

Large Format Printers / Plotter

An extremely specialized type of printer that is designed to print on large sheets or rolls of paper and are commonly used in professional and commercial settings, such as graphic design studios, architectural firms, and print shops.

Scanner

A device that is used to create digital copies of physical documents, images, and other materials. Scanners work by using a light source and sensors to capture the content of the document and convert it into digital data that can be stored on a computer or other digital storage device.

Projector

A device that is used to display images or video onto a large screen or surface. Projectors work by projecting a beam of light onto a surface, typically a screen or a wall, and displaying an image or video onto that surface.

Large Format Display

A type of monitor or screen that is designed to display content on a larger scale than traditional computer monitors and are used for digital signages, advertising, presentations, and video conferencing.

Hospitality TV

A type of television that is specifically designed for use in the hospitality industry, such as hotels, resorts, and cruise ships. Hospitality TVs are equipped with features that cater to the needs of guests, providing a more customized and personalized experience.
