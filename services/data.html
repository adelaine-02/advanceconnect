Data Centers Infrastructure

Precision Airconditioning Unit (PACU)

A specialized air conditioning system used in data centers to maintain precise temperature and humidity levels to ensure the optimal performance and reliability of the IT equipment.

Centralized UPS

A type of power protection system that is commonly used in data centers to provide continuous and reliable power to IT equipment.

Fire Protection (FDAS)

Critical components of data center infrastructure that help to prevent and detect fires within the facility. FDAS systems typically consist of a range of sensors, alarms, and control systems that work together to detect fires as early as possible and alert personnel to the presence of a fire.

Parameter Security (Biometric, CCTV)

Critical component of data center infrastructure that helps to protect the physical assets of the facility. This involves a range of measures to control access to the data center, including security fencing, gates, surveillance cameras, security guards, and other access control systems.

Rack Cabinets

Key component of data center infrastructure that are used to house and organize IT equipment, such as servers, storage devices, switches, and other networking equipment.

Modular Datacenters

Pre-fabricated, self-contained unit that is designed to provide a complete IT infrastructure solution in a compact and portable package, including all the components of a traditional data center.

Civil Works

Construction and installation of the physical foundation, building structure, and supporting systems that are necessary to create a secure, stable, and reliable environment for data center operations.

Electrical Works

Design, installation, and maintenance of the electrical systems that provide power to the data center equipment including UPS, backup generators, and cabling.
